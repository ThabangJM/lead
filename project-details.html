About the Show

Podcast and Chill: Celebrity Edition is a spin-off of Podcast and Chill with MacG, specifically focused on in-depth interviews with South African and international celebrities.

The podcast features in-depth conversations with South African celebrities, influencers, and industry insiders, making it one of the most-watched podcasts in the country
