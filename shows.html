NICHE
PODCAST
These podcasts focus on a particular topic or area of interest that may not be mainstream but has a dedicated and passionate audience. The topics can range from specific hobbies, professions, or genres to more obscure interests that may not have as much representation in mainstream media.
ENTERTAINMENT
PODCAST
An entertainment podcast focuses on humor, featuring comedians or entertaining hosts discussing various topics in a light-hearted manner. Content may include comedy sketches, storytelling, interviews, panel discussions, and improvisation, blending scripted and spontaneous humor to provide laughter and amusement.
MUSIC
PODCAST
A music podcast explores music-related topics, including album reviews, artist interviews, genre discussions, trends, and history. It may feature live performances, music analysis, and insights into the industry, catering to music enthusiasts seeking discovery and deeper understanding.
CONVESATIONAL
PODCAST
A conversational/current affairs podcast discusses recent news and societal topics in an informal style, featuring expert opinions, interviews, and debates. It blends factual reporting with personal insights, making current events engaging and accessible to listeners.
LIFESTYLE
PODCAST
A lifestyle podcast explores topics like health, relationships, fashion, travel, and self-care, offering advice, tips, and insights from experts or enthusiasts. It aims to inspire and provide practical guidance for improving daily life.
OTHER
PODCAST
Dive into our eclectic "Others" category where podcasts defy the ordinary and embrace the unique. This collection is home to shows that break the mold with innovative formats, intriguing topics, and unconventional discussions.

People Need Comedy

The Fun Cast(Bro Beans Studio)

he Fun Cast is a podcast produced by Bro Beans Studios that offers a blend of humor, engaging conversations, and trending topics. Designed to entertain and inspire, the show features a variety of guests, including notable South African personalities

Open Chats Podcast

podcast that delves into various topics relevant to youth culture, dating, and societal issues. The show is known for its relatable and often humorous discussions, providing insights into the experiences and perspectives of South African youth

P&C Celebrity

The Penuel Show

hosted by Penuel The Black Pen, where he shares his thoughts on various topics and engages in conversations with diverse guests

Mr Jazziq Podcast

an Amapiano-focused podcast hosted by South African DJ and record producer Mr JazziQ. Launched in 2022, the podcast aims to shed light on the rapidly growing Amapiano dance genre

Piano Unleashed

Piano Unleashed is a podcast series produced by the Podcast and Chill Network, focusing on live, unplugged performances by prominent artists in the Amapiano genre. The series offers fans an intimate experience, showcasing exclusive renditions of popular tracks and deepening the appreciation for Amapiano music.

Piano Pulse

Piano Pulse is a podcast produced by the Podcast and Chill Network that delves into the vibrant world of Amapiano, a popular South African music genre. Launched in August 2024, the show is hosted by TikTok content creators Thakgi Ledwaba and Munaka Muthambi, along with artist manager Sfiso Ndlovu. Together, they engage in unfiltered discussions about the latest trends, releases, and controversies within the Amapiano scene

City Girls Podcast

This South African lifestyle and talk show, hosted by Tebogo Thobejane, delves into the diverse and dynamic lifestyles of millennial women. The podcast addresses a range of topics pertinent to modern women, fostering engaging discussions and insights.

Black Friday Podcast

The show features interviews with entrepreneurs and business leaders, providing them with a platform to showcase their brands and share their journeys. For instance, episodes have featured guests like Harry from Neoentle Labour Solutions and Sylvester Chauke, CEO of DNA Brand Architects.This initiative aims to support and uplift black-owned enterprises by offering them exposure to a broader audience.

Mzansi Luxury Podcast

Luxury Mzansi Cribs is a real estate reality series hosted by TT Mbha, showcasing some of South Africa's most luxurious homes. The show provides viewers with an exclusive look into high-end properties, highlighting their unique features and designs.

Awkward Dates Podcast

Awkward Dates is a South African YouTube dating show created and hosted by Lasizwe Dambuza. In this unscripted series, Lasizwe embarks on blind dates with various celebrities, leading to unpredictable and humorous interactions. The show aims to provide an entertaining glimpse into the dynamics of first dates, complete with all their inherent awkwardness.

King Oumar Podcast

King Oumar is a South African digital creator and YouTuber known for his engaging and entertaining content. While he doesn't host a dedicated podcast, he frequently appears as a guest on various podcasts and vodcasts, sharing insights into his experiences as a content creator and discussing topics related to the South African digital landscape.

The SMWX Podcast

SMWX (Sizwe Mpofu-Walsh Xperience) is a youth-centered digital current affairs channel that delves into South African politics through in-depth interviews and analysis. Hosted by Dr. Sizwe Mpofu-Walsh, the platform offers a journey into the heart of South African politics, providing insightful discussions on contemporary issues.

On The Table Podcast

On The Table is a South African podcast series that delves into a wide range of topics, including relationships, societal issues, and personal experiences. Hosted by Tshepi Mabs alongside co-hosts Ghost Lady and DJ Black Velvet, the show offers candid discussions and insights pertinent to contemporary life in South Africa.
