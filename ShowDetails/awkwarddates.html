About the Show

"Awkward Dates" is a South African YouTube dating show created and hosted by Lasizwe Dambuza. The show features Lasizwe going on blind dates with various celebrities, engaging in candid and often humorous conversations.

Each episode features unfiltered, candid conversations where Lasizwe and his date explore topics ranging from romance, relationships, personal lives, and pop culture. The show thrives on awkward silences, unexpected confessions, and humorous misunderstandings, making it a blend of comedy, reality dating, and celebrity gossip.
